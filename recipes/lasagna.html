Lasagna

Description

Its a food madeup of some eggs, cheese and burgger

Its a perfect food for those who love fried foods

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Burgger
  • Spaghetti

Steps

  1. Fry some eggs
  2. Add cheese to the fried eggs and allow it to fry for 5 mins
  3. Boil the spaghetti for 10 mins
  4. Add some burgger and add it together with the fried eggs and there you have your lasagna
